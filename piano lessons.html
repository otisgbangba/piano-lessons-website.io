1. Playground Sessions

Playground Sessions is the best tool and software for beginners to advanced piano players.

If you want to learn the piano with a program that is in line with today's technology, this is it!

What separates it from other programs is the ability to learn the piano through playing your favourite songs.

It's recommended for those who are just starting out, however it's also a great program for anyone who's already playing the piano, and who just wants to enhance their playing skills.

The program is set up in 3 different level categories, and there are different levels within those, making all the lessons applicable to anyone.

The software allows you to play and learn your favourite songs, understand music theory, and watch video lessons from an awesome teacher.

You can access the lessons anytime you want, and the program is built in with a feedback and scoring system which helps with your progress. So it has that game aspect, while at the same time real in-person video lessons and learning.

This is better than any app, or music learning website. It's really the only tool you need to start improving your playing skills.

  • This is for EVERYONE

    The lessons are designed for all skill levels. Whether you’re just starting out, or you’ve been taking lessons for years, you will benefit from this program.

  • Do I Need the Internet to Play?

    You need to first download the software on your PC or Mac. You also need to be hooked up to the internet to download any songs or lessons, and participate in the online community. However, once you download a song or lesson, you DON’T need to be online to use the software.

  • What Kind of Piano Do I Need?

    You can use this program with a keyboard or a traditional piano. You only need a computer to access the video lessons and songs. From there you can play and practice on any type of piano. In order to get instant feedback as you play, you will need a keyboard with a USB or midi connection, but the core program and lessons can be used on any “type” of piano.
